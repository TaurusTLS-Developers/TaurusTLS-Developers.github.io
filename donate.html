Donate to TaurusTLS

TaurusTLS is—and will always remain—a 100% free, open-source project. Up until now, the out-of-pocket costs required to maintain the library have been covered personally by the maintainers. Setting up an OpenCollective gives our community a transparent way to help sustain the project financially.

All funds collected will be used strictly to cover project infrastructure and code-quality expenses, such as:

  • Website and documentation hosting
  • Purchasing necessary development, testing, or profiling tools

A note on transparency: Financial support is entirely voluntary. You do not need to donate to use TaurusTLS, and contributing financially does not grant special consideration or expedited feature requests. Whether you support us through code contributions, bug reports, or a cup of coffee on OpenCollective, we deeply appreciate you being part of the TaurusTLS community!
